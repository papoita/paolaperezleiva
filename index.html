About Me

Paola Pérez Leiva has a background in Microbiology and a MSc in Environmental Sciences from ETH Zürich. She has worked in Environmental Research from biological, social and technical perspectives holding positions in Bioremediation, Molecular Biology, Sustainable Development, Resource Management and Remote Sensing. She is passionate about using her interdisciplinary training to make science more accessible.
